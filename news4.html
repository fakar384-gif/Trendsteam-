First Human Colony on Mars by 2030

📅 Published on: April 28, 2026 | Category: Space

Space agencies have officially signed an agreement to start building the first human settlement on Mars. The initial phase will involve sending robotic rovers to set up essential infrastructure.

Scientists believe that this mission will be a giant leap for mankind, making us an interplanetary species. The first batch of astronauts is expected to leave Earth by late 2029.
